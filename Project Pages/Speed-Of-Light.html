Speed of Light Measurement

Experimental Measurement of the Speed of Light Using an Oscilloscope

Overview

In this project, I designed and executed an experiment to measure the speed of light using lab equipment accessible to university students. The focus was on translating theoretical knowledge into practical measurement and understanding the limitations of instruments like oscilloscopes and function generators.
